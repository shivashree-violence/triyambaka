About TRIYAMBAKA

TRIYAMBAKA

THE THREE-EYED AI SURVEILLANCE SYSTEM

TRIYAMBAKA is named after the three-eyed form of Lord Shiva — the all-seeing divine eye that misses nothing. Just as Shiva's third eye sees all threats and destroys evil, TRIYAMBAKA uses AI vision to detect violence in real-time surveillance feeds.

It is a free, open-access AI-powered violence detection system that analyzes live camera feeds, uploaded videos, and images for signs of violence or threatening behavior using Google Gemini Vision AI.

Built as a college final year project using Python, Flask, OpenCV, and Google Gemini Vision API.

TECHNOLOGIES USED

BACKEND
Python 3, Flask, OpenCV, PIL
AI ENGINE
Google Gemini 1.5 Flash Vision
VIDEO STREAM
